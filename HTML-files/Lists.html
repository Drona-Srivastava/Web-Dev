Maggie Recipe

  1. Take a pan
  2. Take 200mL of water in the pan
  3. Boil the water
  4. Add Maggie Tastemaker
  5. Mix the masala
  6. Add Maggie noodles
  7. Cook for atleast 5 mins
  8. Add a spoon of red chilli powder
  9. Add a small cube of salted butter (preffered Amul)
  10. Let the butter melt mix in the red chilli powder
  11. Serve it hot
